About Me

I'm Wiktor, and I'm 18 years old. I've been realizing my passion for creating games and bringing ideas to life for nearly 6 years, though game mechanics and their inner works have interested me for as long as I can remember. I started by experimenting with Pygame in Python, but quickly moved into creating games in Unity, which has become the engine of my choice.

I love designing games, implementing my ideas, and seeing people have fun playing them. My portfolio is a mix of game jam projects and larger-scale games alongside my Factorio modification, which has reached over 20,000 downloads. I've also created two tabletop prototypes to explore game design in a physical format.

I feel confident in what I do because I genuinely love it. I am driven to build a future in the games industry, and I’m looking for an internship to face new challenges and gain professional knowledge from an experienced team.
